About Me

I am a freelance 3D modeler specializing in using Blender to create models and assets for video games. The field of 3D modeling for video games is a dynamic and challenging environment in which I have made my way with my passion for design and technical expertise.

My experience includes the creation of a variety of video game assets, including objects, environments, and user interface elements. Each model requires careful planning and attention to detail, along with a solid understanding of the needs of the game and its technical restrictions.

Working as a freelance 3D modeler has also allowed me to adapt quickly to changes in the industry, staying up-to-date on new technologies and trends.
